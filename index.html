Origins of Hi-Chew

Hi-Chew is a Japanese fruit chew, first released in 1975. The candy is known for its unique, chewy texture and wide variety of flavors.

Hi-Chew was invented by Taichiro Morinaga, the founder of Morinaga & Company, with the idea of creating a chewy, edible gum that could be swallowed.
